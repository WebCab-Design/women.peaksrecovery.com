Medicaid Providers Colorado

Peaks Recovery Centers are NOT Medicaid providers. That being said, we believe in getting people to the appropriate level of care and have provided a list of Medicaid/ free providers below within the state of Colorado. All information presented is to the best of our knowledge. There may be further resources that we are not aware of at this time.
