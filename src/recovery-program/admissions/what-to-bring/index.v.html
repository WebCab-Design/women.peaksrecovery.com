Serenity Peaks Recovery Center - List of Items to Bring

Serenity Peaks Recovery Center is located in Colorado Springs, Colorado and experiences all the seasons. The staff at Peaks Recovery highly recommend packing accordingly when preparing to come to treatment. Peaks Recovery clients will also participate in outdoor activities as well as going to the gym. It is advised that the client brings approximately 2-3 weeksâ€™ worth of clothes, including:

Clothing

  • Regular socks and a few pairs of wool socks for hiking
  • Underwear
  • Swimsuit
  • Gym clothes/ active wear
  • Comfortable pants and t-shirts/ loungewear/ pajamas
  • Short sleeved t-shirts and long-sleeved t-shirts
  • Tank tops
  • Sweatshirts/ sweaters
  • Pairs of jeans

Outerwear

  • Warm Winter Coat (Months: November - April)
  • Warm hat/ beanies (Months: November - April)

  • Long Underwear - fleece lined or thermal

Shoes

  • Sandals
  • Hiking Boots
  • Winter/ Snow Boots (Months: November - April)

Personal Hygiene

  • Shampoo/ Conditioner
  • Hairbrush
  • Body/ Bath Soap
  • Deoderant/ Antiperspirant
  • Toothbrush/ Toothpaste
  • Sealed Multi-Vitamins

Miscellaneous Items

  • Personal Keepsakes and Pictures
  • Blanked and Pillows - If Desired (Otherwise we provide these items)
  • Personal Electronics (e.g. phone and tablet). Client will have use of these items after approximately sixty (60) days and upon staff approval.

Items/ Clothing NOT allowed

  • Money
  • Cologne
  • Contraband - no weapons of any kind
  • Open supplements, herbs, laxatives or diuretics
  • Mouthwash (anything containing alcohol)
